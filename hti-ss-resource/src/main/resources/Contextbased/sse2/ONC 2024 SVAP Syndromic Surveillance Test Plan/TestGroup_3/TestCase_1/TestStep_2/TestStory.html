Description

At 9:00 AM the next day, June 18, 2025, the patient is visited by the surgeon and an attending physician who perform a physical to check for signs and symptoms of any post-operation compilations. The patient reports that he is experiencing chest pain. The physicians both observe that the patient has signs of cardiac arrhythmia (I49.9). However, the patient suddenly suffers a pulmonary embolism and dies at 12pm. The pulmonary embolism (ICD-10 I26 Pulmonary embolism) is documented as a final diagnosis.

NIST Hospital reports syndromic surveillance data to the State Public Health Department (NIST PH SS FAC 2) at least once per day. At 12:30 PM on June 18, 2025, the hospital's electronic health record module for syndromic surveillance data assembles and transmits an ADT 08 Update message about this encounter. The patient name and birthdate are de-identified in the submission, so no date of birth is included, and the name is represented with name type 'S' to indicate that the name is empty for anonymity.


Comments

The dates and times in this test case illustrate the sequence of clinical and messaging events. Since the exact dates and times are not reproducible when modeling the test case with an HIT Module, only date and time format will be validated within tester submitted test data.


Pre-condition

A04-Registration message is sent before the A08-Update message.


Post-Condition

No Post-Condition
